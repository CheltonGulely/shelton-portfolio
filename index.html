About me

Shelton Gulely

Hi, I am Shelton Gulely and I was born and raised in southern Africa, Mozambique.
From an early age, I have always been passionate about computers. Actually, the first time I used a computer was to play video games when I was about six or so. Seriously, playing video games was pretty entertaining; however, I also realized that it was not the best use of my time. After this realization, I decided to shift my focus to use my time on something more beneficial on the long run. In the beginning, I found it really hard to get rid of my old habits but then after a few days I started wondering, why don’t I learn how to code and design video games instead? It all seemed so complicated and I didn’t even know where to start because I had never heard of anyone who knew how to code in my neighborhood to begin with. Thankfully, I knew YouTube had to have something about coding, and as it turns out, after doing a quick search I realized that there were courses on coding by the bazillions. This marked the beginning of my passion for coding.

I started watching tutorials on YouTube day after day, and my passion for coding grew even stronger as I saw my skills build up each day. Sometimes I came across content that I could barely understand, until I found a YouTuber who was selling courses on coding. The courses were so intriguing and well put together, so I decided to buy one of his courses with classes on full stack development, which turned out to be one of the best decisions I have ever made because not only did the course allow me to improve my coding skills, but it also exposed me to whole range of coding fields, and I eventually fell in love with web development. From that time onwards, I have been working on a big web development project. Click here if you have any questions and or if you want to connect with me directly.
